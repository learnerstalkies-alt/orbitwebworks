Common Questions

Everything You
Need to Know

How long does it take to build a Business website? +
Most Business websites are delivered in 10–14 business days from the approved design. Landing pages can be delivered in 5–7 days. Timelines depend on how quickly you provide content (logo, photos, copy) and feedback.
What do you need from me to get started? +
We need your logo (or we can create one), high-quality food and venue photos, your menu, and a brief about your brand and target customers. We guide you through everything in the discovery call — no experience needed.
Will my website rank on Google? +
Every website we build includes a solid SEO foundation: proper meta tags, fast loading, structured data, mobile optimisation, and local SEO setup. Ongoing ranking takes time and content — we offer monthly SEO plans if you want to accelerate this.
How many revisions are included? +
All projects include 3 rounds of revisions at the design stage and 2 rounds after development. We find this is more than enough for 95% of projects. Additional revisions can be added at a flat rate.
Can I update the website myself after it's built? +
Yes — we can build your site with a simple CMS (like Sanity or a custom admin panel) so you can update menus, photos, and content without touching code. Alternatively, our Monthly Growth Plan handles all updates for you.
Do you work with Business outside India? +
Absolutely. We work with Business and hospitality brands globally. All communication is in English and we adapt pricing to local markets. Book a free consultation to discuss your project.
